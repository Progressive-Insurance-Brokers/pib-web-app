SHORT-TERM INSURANCE

Progressive insurance brokers offers the following broad classes of insurance;

  • Motor
  • Agriculture
  • Property
  • Casulty
  • Engineering
  • Marine
  • Miscellaneous accidents
  • Bonds and guarantee

Detailed below is a summary of cover provided;

1. Motor Vehicle Insurance

Your car is an indispensable part of your life. Consider the many potential risks that your car is exposed to and ensure that it is sufficiently protected.

Vehicle insurance cover options:

  • Road Traffic Act
  • Full third party
  • Full third party – Fire and theft
  • Comprehensive

Cover provided for:

  • Private motor – limited to 9 vehicles
  • Motor fleet - suitable for companies; minimum 20 vehicles
  • Motor traders
  • Motor cycles & trailers
  • Commercial vehicles – such as trucks, staff buses, school buses, commuter omnibus etc

2. Agriculture Insurance

Over the years we have built-up a close relationship with our farming clients. The agricultural community demands a personal and reliable service and we pride ourselves in doing exactly that! We know your farm is important to you, regardless its nature or size. As your partner in agriculture, we aim to make insurance even more accessible, affordable and appealing. We continue to invest in effective risk management in order to make a real difference in helping you create a sustainable future.

Farmers Master policy

Cover designed to provide comprehensive cover for farmer;

  • Fire on farm buildings & contents, crops and farmhouse and contents
  • All risks (business property)
  • Livestock
  • Theft and money
  • Personal accident and personal all risks

3. Property Insurance (Home contents)

It is easy to underestimate the value of your home’s contents. Sadly, this can be an expensive oversight should something ever go wrong. We are here to help you make sure your home contents cover is sufficient.

Home combined policy

This is a package for householders and house-owners. The cover is for:

  • Buildings, contents, clothing and personal effect (all risks)
  • Personal accidents
  • Personal liabilities as owners/ occupiers in personal capacity
  • Comprehensive

Property insurance cover is in respect of loss or damage to tangible assets or property excluding motor vehicles against risk of fire, lightning, riot strike, explosion, storm damage, earthquake. Property insured ranges from dwelling house to large industry complexes including contents.

4. Casulties

Cover includes;

  • Liabilities – cover against legal liabilities to 3rd parties due to negligence by insured causing injury to other parties (general liability, products liability & detective workmanship and common law & employer’s liability)
  • Personal accident – cover for accidental death or bodily injury including incurred medical expenses caused by violent, external and visible means
  • Fidelity guarantee

Property insurance cover is in respect of loss or damage to tangible assets or property excluding motor vehicles against risk of fire, lightning, riot strike, explosion, storm damage, earthquake. Property insured ranges from dwelling house to large industry complexes including contents.

5. Engineering

Cover for sudden & unforeseen physical loss or damage to plant & machinery also includes construction projects.

  • Contractors all risk
  • Plan all risks
  • Erection all risks
  • Electronic equipment
  • Machinery breakdown

7. Marine

Cover for sudden & unforeseen physical loss or damage to plant & machinery also includes construction projects.

  • Cargo Cover for accidental loss or damage to goods whilst in transit by road, rail, air or sea

9. Bonds & guarantees
