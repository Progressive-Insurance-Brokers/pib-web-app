About Us

Progressive Insurance brokers has been a dominant player in the insurance industry for over 35 years, it is a wholly Zimbabwean owned company. We provide short-term and life insurance as well as investment products to a diverse customer base, including individual, commercial entities and corporate clients.

Why choose us?

We thrive on building lasting relationships, no matter the loss you experience we are with our clients throughout. Progressive Insurance brokers prides on its professionalism and integrity as we value our clients peace of mind.

Our role as insurance brokers

You may rightly wonder what an insurance broker does. An insurance broker’s business is to provide a one stop professional shop for you where we make it our business to identify insurance products and services that meet your needs at a fee and terms that recognize your individual circumstances. At PIB we recognize that making financial investment decisions in an industry with multiple players who all offer seemingly similar insurance products is a complex and invariably frustrating experience. That is where we come in.


We make it our business to analyze all the financial products (policies) on offer in the country in terms of product design, rates, underwriting requirements, bonuses, claims performances and the company’s financial viability. From this analysis we are able to select the most appropriate and beneficial products for you. Therefore as brokers we make an otherwise tedious experience pleasant and rewarding. We have no doubt you will find the confidence and satisfaction of dealing with us the experts in this field priceless.
